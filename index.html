What is WinterHell?


WinterHell is a Minecraft Bedrock/Pocket Edition Network Consisting of Games.
We try our best to provide an amazing player experience.
We always take player suggestions before adding anything in-game,
by taking suggestions in our discord server

KitPvP is a PvP game mode with a twist. Players have to choose a kit from the kit
shop and jump down to the arena to fight amongst other players.
By killing other players you earn coins that can be used to purchase
more powerful kits. To WinterHell KitPvP we addedour twist by
targeting all kinds of player base such as campers, sweats, fantasy lovers.
